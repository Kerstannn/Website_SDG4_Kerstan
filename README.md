## Website Structure

The website has been divided into several main sections, each with specific content to provide a comprehensive overview of the hackathon:

- **Header**:
Displays the event title and includes a call-to-action button for registration.

- **Hero Section**:
Features a navigation bar with links to the different sections of the website.

- **About**:
Provides information about the hackathon’s objectives and goals related to urban heat and flood resilience.

- **SDG 13 Connection**:
Explains how the event aligns with **SDG 13: Climate Action** and its significance in addressing climate challenges.

- **Event Schedule**:
Lists important dates and milestones for the hackathon.

- **Speakers & Mentors**:
Showcases profiles of the speakers and mentors involved in the event.

- **Tracks & Challenges**:
Describes the various challenges participants will tackle during the hackathon, such as heat mitigation and flood resilience.

- **Resources**:
Provides links to external resources related to climate action and SDG 13.

- **Sponsors & Partners**:
Displays logos of the event’s sponsors and partners.

- **Registration**:
A simple form for users to sign up for the event.

- **Footer**:
Contains contact information and a link to SDG 13 details.



## Features

- **Responsive Design**:
The layout adapts to various screen sizes. The navigation menu and other sections stack and resize based on the device width.

- **Event Information**:
Clear and easy-to-navigate sections with essential details about the hackathon, including the schedule, challenges, and goals.

- **Registration Form**:
A basic registration form that asks for the participant's name, email, and interest in the event.

- **External Resources**:
Links to relevant external websites and resources related to SDG 13 and climate action.

- **Sponsors Section**:
A section to show sponsor logos (using placeholder images for the assignment).

- **Mobile Responsiveness**:
I also focused on making the website **responsive**, ensuring it works well across different devices like desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Using **media queries**, I adjusted font sizes, padding, and layout to provide a seamless experience for users on any screen size.
